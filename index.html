Blockchain Evidence Framework

Cryptographic
Evidence Ledger

Tamper-proof, SHA-256 hashed chain-of-custody for all water quality readings. NFC-tagged samples with cryptographic timestamps. Community-verifiable integrity.

How the Evidence Chain Works

1
📡

IoT Sensor / Monitor Submits

Sentinel station or community monitor records water quality parameters (Hg, Se, Pb, pH, DO).

→
2
🏷️

NFC Tag & Timestamp

Sample tagged with cryptographic NFC seal. ISO 8601 timestamp recorded.

→
3
🔐

SHA-256 Hash Generated

Block data + previous hash → SHA-256 digest creates immutable chain link.

→
4
✅

Community Verification

Anyone can verify chain integrity by recomputing all hashes. Tampering detected instantly.

Submit Water Quality Reading

Readings are SHA-256 hashed and appended to the immutable evidence chain. All entries are timestamped and linked to previous block.

Chain Integrity Verification

Recompute all SHA-256 hashes and verify each block's link to its predecessor. Any tampering will be detected and flagged.

NFC-Tagged Chain of Custody

Each physical sample is sealed with an NFC tag generating a unique cryptographic ID. The custody trail is recorded on-chain.

🧪

Sample Collection

Field collection at sentinel station. GPS coordinates + ISO 8601 timestamp logged.

→
🏷️

NFC Seal Applied

Tamper-evident NFC tag affixed. Tag writes: sample ID, collector, coordinates, time.

→
🚚

Transport Logged

Each custody transfer (collection → transport → lab) logged with NFC tap and timestamped.

→
🔬

Lab Analysis

NABL-accredited lab receives, scans NFC, confirms chain-of-custody, runs AAS analysis.

→
🔗

On-Chain Record

Results hashed with full custody trail. Immutable block appended to evidence chain.
